This is the life these individuals are facing every day. Waking up without a safe or stable place to call home, many struggle to find basic necessities such as food and water. Nights are often spent on the streets, exposed to harsh weather and unsafe conditions. Access to healthcare is limited, and untreated illnesses or injuries can quickly escalate and become life threatening. Simple tasks that most of us take for granted such as eating and bathing, become constant challenges. On top of these physical hardships, social isolation and stigma makes daily life mentally and emotionally exhausting. Homelessness is not just a lack of housing. It is a constant and relentless battle against hunger, vulnerability, and uncertainty. Every day underscores the urgent need for support, resources, and community action to help these individuals regain stability, safety, and hope.
